About Us

First-Call Medical, Inc., is an Independent Diagnostic Testing Facility (IDTF) specializing in the review and analysis of ECGs from Digital Holter and Long-Term Holter Monitoring, Wireless Cardiac Event Monitoring, and Mobile Cardiac Telemetry Services. We also offer Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring services.
Established in 1989, First-Call Medical, Inc. was founded to meet the cardiac monitoring service needs of independent practitioners, hospitals, and clinics nationwide. With over 30 years of experience in the industry, we have earned a reputation as a reliable service provider.

At First-Call Medical, Inc., we understand the importance of timely and accurate reporting in the healthcare field. That’s why our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every ECG analysis is performed with precision.

First-Call Medical, Inc. is committed to providing uninterrupted service to our clients. Whether you’re a healthcare provider in need of cardiac monitoring services or a patient seeking diagnostic testing, you can trust First-Call Medical, Inc. to deliver exceptional care.

Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can assist you with your cardiac monitoring needs.
