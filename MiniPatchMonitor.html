5-In-1 Mini Patch Monitor

The RX-1 mini is a revolutionary patch-style remote cardiac monitor that offers an unparalleled level of flexibility, customization, and accuracy in collecting and presenting data on a patient’s heart activity. Its innovative design provides three distinct modes of operation.

The first mode is auto-trigger mode. The monitor detects and records cardiac events based on preset thresholds.

The second mode is the patient-trigger mode. This mode allows the patient to initiate a recording whenever they experience symptoms.

The third mode is heart rate trend monitoring. This mode continuously tracks the patient’s heart rate over an extended period, unveiling patterns and trends that may not manifest as distinct episodes but could still indicate underlying issues.

By seamlessly combining data from all three of these modes, the RX-1 mini offers clinicians an extraordinarily comprehensive, multi-dimensional view of their patients’ cardiac health. This empowers more precise diagnoses and treatment plans tailored to each person’s unique conditions and needs. With its powerful yet user-friendly monitoring capabilities, the RX-1 mini stands to transform the landscape of remote cardiac care and disease management.

Monitor Highlights

Designed for unparalleled convenience and accuracy, this innovative system features a small, water-tight patch device that is worn directly on the patient’s chest, transmitting two channels of ECG data via a built-in cell and Wi-Fi modems. Unlike traditional Holter monitors, there are no cumbersome patient cables – just a single, easy-to-operate button and an exceptionally long-lasting rechargeable battery that powers the device for up to 5 days on a single charge. The patch is simple to apply and can even be worn in the shower, promoting high patient compliance and satisfaction. Moreover, the system boasts unprecedented noise suppression capabilities and a best-in-class device algorithm that performs an initial analysis. This is combined with artificial intelligence and review by certified cardiac technicians to analyze every heartbeat, ensuring the final report is comprehensive and accurate. With its seamless usability and advanced multi-layered analysis, this wearable ECG solution represents a groundbreaking advancement in remote cardiac monitoring.

Ordering Process

Our Home-Enrollment program is our most popular service and only requires one form from your office. To enroll a patient with this service, please click on our Patient Enrollment Form . Once finished, click submit, and the order will be securely sent to our office. First-Call Medical will verify insurance, contact the patient, and send a monitor directly to their home or office. Most orders are processed within an hour, and monitors arrive at the patient’s home within two business days.

The other service we offer is the In-Office Hookup Service, which allows you to provide your patients with cardiac monitors during their appointments. First-Call medical equipment will be left at your center.

We will train you on the monitor application.

Reports

We offer a secure website for report access 24 hours a day. Physicians can review and print a patient file from any internet connection.
