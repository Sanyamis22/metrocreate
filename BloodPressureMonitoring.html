24-Hour Blood Pressure Monitoring

The Spacelabs Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitor is a diagnostic tool utilized by renal transplant and cardiology centers across the country. Recordings are initiated at specific times, as directed by a physician, and all data is stored within the monitor. The monitor is worn for 24 hours, initiating one to four recordings per hour. All diary entries are entered in the final report.

First-Call Medical is currently a contracted service provider to many Renal Transplant Programs in the United States.

For more information, please call 800-274-5399.
